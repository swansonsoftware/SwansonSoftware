Lobster and Asparagus Penne Pasta


PREP: ? min; Cooking time: ? min
Serves 4



  1   lb. Lobster meat cut in ½ inch chunks
  1   lb. Raw shrimp
  1   lb. Penne pasta, cooked
  4   oz. Asparagus, blanched (approx. 24 spears)
  1   15 oz. jar four-cheese alfredo sauce
  2   tbsp. Land O Lakes Roasted Garlic Butter spread
    Fresh grated Parmesan cheese

1.   Prepare asparagus by cutting off wood ends. Cut into 1 inch pieces.
 
2.   Place asparagus in boiling salted water for 2 minutes. Drain.
 
3.   Cook penne pasta according to directions on box. Drain.
 
4.   In hot saucepan, melt 2 tbsp. Roasted garlic butter.
 
5.   Add lobster meat and shrimp, cooking until shrimp is opaque.
 
6.   Add asparagus, pasta and Alfredo sauce, cooking until everything is heated through.
 
7.   Serve, garnish with grated Parmesan cheese

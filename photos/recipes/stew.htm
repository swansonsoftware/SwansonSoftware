Beef Stew
Modified from Betty Crocker, 2000.

PREP: 15 min; Bake: 25 min
Serves 6

Try using different types of diced tomatoes and frozen vegetables. If you’re using Italian-style tomatoes, add 1/2 teaspoon dried basil with the baking mix.

  1   pound ground beef
  3   cups water
  1   can (28 ounces) diced tomatoes (any variety), undrained
  1   can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  1   bag (16 ounces) frozen vegetables
  2 ¼   cups Bisquick® Original baking mix
  2/3   cup milk
  ½   teaspoon dried basil

1.   Heat oven to 425°. Cook beef in ovenproof Dutch oven over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until brown; drain. Stir in water, tomatoes, tomato paste, vegetables, and basil. Heat to full boil, stirring occasionally; remove from heat.
 
2.   Stir baking mix and milk until soft dough forms. Drop by 6 spoonfuls onto beef mixture.
 
3.   Bake uncovered 20 to 25 minutes or until biscuits are golden brown and stew is bubbly.
 
1 Serving: 410 calories

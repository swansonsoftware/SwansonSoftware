Flavored Water
by

PREP: 10 min; Cooking time: none
Serves

This is a simple drink that is healthy as well as refreshing

  10   thin slices of cucumber (any variety)
  2   thin slices of lemon
  10   bing cherries, halved and pitted
  4   strawberries, quartered
  2   quarts filtered water (substitute mineral water or club soda)
    optional: other fruit

1.   Put filtered water and ingredients in a glass caraf, and chill in the refrigerator.
 
  Serve chilled

Lamb Stew


PREP: 20 min; Cooking time: 2 ½ hrs.
Serves 6



  1 ½   lb lamb shoulder or sirloin
  10   cloves garlic
  1   cup diced carrots
  ¼   cup diced onion
  1   cup diced celery
  1   bay leaf
  1   14 oz can Italian-style tomatoes; crush with hands to squeeze out juice, then strain out juice and save
  1   cup red wine
  1   sprig rosemary
  ½   teaspoon salt
  ½   teaspoon pepper

1.   In large bowl, put carrots, celery, onions, garlic, bayleaf, rosemary, and red wine. Put the meat on top, and put in refrigerator overnight.
 
2.   Remove the meat from marinade and set aside. Strain the red wine from the vegetables and save. Set vegetables aside.
 
3.   Brown the meat on all sides in a deep pan with a little olive oil. Remove and set aside.
 
4.   Put the vegetables in the pan and saute with the wine. Add meat and tomatoe juice and tomatoes, bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer and cover. Cook for 2 ½ hours.
 
5.   Meat should fall apart easily when done. Use meat and sauce as topping for pasta.

Spicy Shrimp


PREP: 15 min; Cooking time: 15 min
Serves 4



  2   tablespoons light olive oil (substitute butter)
  6   garlic cloves (or however much you like), finely chopped
  2   pounds uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  4   large carrots, cut into julienne strips (2 cups)
  2   green onions, chopped
  ¼   cup cilantro, chopped
  1   (optional) teaspoon red pepper flakes

1.   Add the oil to a large skillet and saute garlic and carrots over medium heat for four or five minutes.
 
2.   Add shrimp, green onions, and red pepper flakes, stir-fry 5 to 10 minutes or until shrimp are pink and firm and carrots are almost tender. Stir in cilantro.
 
    Serve over rice.
 
Serves 4
